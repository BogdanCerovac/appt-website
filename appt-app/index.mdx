# Appt app

Almost half of all people with a mobile phone use accessibility features. Much more than you would expect, right?

Unfortunately, most apps do not take these accessibility features into account. As a result, millions of people are unable to use apps.

Download the Appt app and learn how to make your app accessible!

[Download Appt app for Android](https://appt.li/android)

[Download Appt app for iOS](https://appt.li/ios)
